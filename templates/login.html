Why does Unspamify need access to my email?

Unspamify is committed to helping busy individuals safeguard their email addresses from unwanted appearances on B2B databases. Here's how our process is designed to serve you:

  • Opt-Out Requests: Once you sign up with Unspamify, we proactively submit opt-out forms on your behalf to various data vendors such as Apollo, Zoominfo, and others. The goal is to ensure your email address is removed from these databases, thereby safeguarding your personal data from potential misuse.
  • Verification Process: After we've initiated the opt-out request, many data vendors send a verification email to the user's address to confirm the removal request. This step is where our email access becomes essential.
  • Domain-Specific Access: Our technology is tailored to scan only for specific verification emails from B2B databases. We only have visibility on emails coming from domains that are listed on unspamify.com. All other emails remain entirely private and beyond our access.
  • Automatic Verification: Once we detect these specific verification emails, our non-invasive technology takes over to automatically complete the verification process on your behalf. This ensures your email address is removed efficiently without requiring manual intervention from you.

Rest assured, your trust and privacy are paramount to us. While we request this specific access to ensure swift verification, your other email content remains untouched, unseen, and unequivocally yours.
